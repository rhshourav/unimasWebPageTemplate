Our Story

A Journey of Excellence in Sportswear Manufacturing

Who We Are

Unimas Sportswear Ltd. has been a pioneer in sportswear manufacturing since 1999. In a significant milestone, Mainland Holdings Ltd. acquired the majority of shares on March 13, 2023, bringing global expertise and resources to enhance our manufacturing capabilities.

Infrastructure

  • Total Factory Area: 708,399 sq. ft
  • Production Area: 374,440 sq. ft
  • Dedicated NE Area: 113,500 sq. ft (2 Floors)
  • FGWH & Warehouse: 140,037 sq. ft
  • Dormitory Area: 31,430 sq. ft

Capacity

  • Total Workforce: 6,999 employees
  • Monthly Headwear Production: ~6,000,000 pieces
  • Monthly Belts & Wallets: 2,000,000 pieces

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, high-quality sportswear solutions while maintaining the highest standards of manufacturing excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in sportswear manufacturing, known for our innovation, quality, and commitment to sustainable practices.

Our Certifications

SEDEX

Enabling responsible supply chains through ethical data exchange and improved supply chain practices

Fair Labor

Committed to maintaining fair working conditions, wages, and labor practices across our operations

C-TPAT

FDC

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Unimas Sportswear, we believe in responsible manufacturing. Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in every aspect of our operations:

  • Use of eco-friendly materials
  • Energy-efficient manufacturing processes
  • Waste reduction and recycling programs
  • Water conservation initiatives
  • Fair labor practices
