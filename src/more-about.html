Our Story

A Journey of Excellence in Sportswear Manufacturing

Who We Are

Unimas Sportswear Ltd. has been a pioneer in sportswear manufacturing since 1999. In a significant milestone, Mainland Holdings Ltd. acquired the majority of shares on March 13, 2023, bringing global expertise and resources to enhance our manufacturing capabilities.

Infrastructure

  • Total Factory Area: 708,399 sq. ft
  • Production Area: 374,440 sq. ft
  • Dedicated NE Area: 113,500 sq. ft (2 Floors)
  • FGWH & Warehouse: 140,037 sq. ft
  • Dormitory Area: 31,430 sq. ft

Capacity

  • Total Workforce: 6,999 employees
  • Monthly Headwear Production: ~6,000,000 pieces
  • Monthly Belts & Wallets: 2,000,000 pieces
