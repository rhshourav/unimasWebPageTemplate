Unimas Sportswear Ltd. subsidiary of

Unimas Sportswear Ltd. is a leading cap manufacturing factory in Bangladesh, established in 1999. With state-of-the-art facilities spanning over 708,399 sq. ft, we specialize in producing premium headwear, belts, and wallets. Our monthly production capacity exceeds 6 million pieces of headwear and 2 million pieces of belts & wallets, making us a significant player in the global accessories industry.
